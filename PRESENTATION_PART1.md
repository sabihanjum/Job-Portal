## SLIDE 2: INTRODUCTION

### What is AI-Powered Job Portal?
An intelligent recruitment platform that leverages artificial intelligence to revolutionize the hiring process by:
- Automating resume parsing and analysis
- Providing semantic job-candidate matching
- Offering personalized career guidance
- Streamlining the entire recruitment workflow

### Problem Statement
Traditional recruitment faces challenges:
- Manual resume screening is time-consuming
- Subjective candidate evaluation
- Difficulty in finding the right job-candidate fit
- Lack of transparency in hiring decisions
- Limited career guidance for job seekers

### Our Solution
An AI-driven platform that:
- Automates resume parsing with 95%+ accuracy
- Uses semantic matching for intelligent job recommendations
- Provides explainable AI for transparent decisions
- Offers role-specific AI assistants for guidance
- Includes skills assessment and interview scheduling
